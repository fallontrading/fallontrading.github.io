Fallon Trading Co., Inc. has been in business since 1999 offering quality fruit juice concentrates, purees, frozen fruit and essences from multiple worldwide premium sources. We offer certified organic concentrates and purees from Turkey, Argentina, South Africa, USA and Canada. We currently sell to a variety of branded and private label customers in the USA, as well as products of the USA to other countries.

Our Staff has more than 25 years in the juice industry with experience in manufacturing, sales and quality of juice concentrates and fruit products both, as a buyer and seller.

We pride ourselves in offering the best service possible. Our objective is to achieve a perfect three-point landing - what you need, where you need it, when you need it - every time!
We warehouse a variety of products in the USA to service our growing customers' needs.
